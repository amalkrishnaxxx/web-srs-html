1 Introduction
1.1 Purpose

This document describes the Software Requirements Specification (SRS) for the Water Filter Company Management System. The purpose is to outline the functional, non-functional, and technical requirements for developing a comprehensive web and mobile application that helps manage customer data, service scheduling, automated reminders, and detailed reporting efficiently.

1.2 Scope

The system will provide end-to-end management of water filter service operations including:

  • Complete customer data management with service history tracking
  • Intelligent service scheduling with automated reminders
  • WhatsApp-based customer notifications
  • Digital service orders with signature capture
  • Comprehensive reporting and analytics
  • Mobile access for field technicians

The system will be used primarily by company staff (Administrators and Technicians) and may optionally provide customer-facing features for service detail viewing.

4 Detailed Functional Requirements
4.1 Dashboard
  • Today's Schedule: Display all customers scheduled for service today with technician assignments.
  • Upcoming Reminders: Show customers due for service in the next 7-14 days.
  • Pending Jobs: Track incomplete or in-progress service orders.
  • Analytics: Summary statistics including total customers, services completed this month, average service interval, and revenue metrics.
  • Quick Actions: Fast access to create new customer, schedule service, or view reports.
4.2 Customer Management
  • Customer Profile: Comprehensive information including Name, Phone Number, Way No., House No., Location/Address.
  • System Details: Water filter model, installation date, warranty information.
  • Search & Filter: Advanced search by name, phone, location, or filter model.
  • Service History: Complete chronological record of all services performed.
  • Notes & Comments: Add custom notes about customer preferences or special requirements.
  • Contact Management: Multiple contact numbers, email addresses.
4.3 Service Orders (Digital Job Cards)
  • Order Details: Unique Service ID, Date & Time, Assigned Technician.
  • Service Information: Complaint nature, work performed, spare parts used with quantities.
  • Consumables Tracking: Salt added (Yes/No with quantity), filters replaced, other parts.
  • Cost Breakdown: Service charges, parts cost, total amount, payment status.
  • Digital Signatures: Capture signatures from both technician and customer on mobile/tablet.
  • Photo Documentation: Attach before/after photos of the service.
  • Auto-Scheduling: Automatically calculate and schedule next service date (default 5 months).
  • Status Tracking: Pending, In Progress, Completed, Cancelled states.
4.4 Reminder & Notification System
  • Automated Triggers: WhatsApp messages sent automatically on scheduled reminder dates.
  • Message Customization: Include service due date, customer name, contact info, and company branding.
  • Multi-channel Support: Fallback to SMS or Email if WhatsApp delivery fails.
  • Delivery Tracking: Log all notifications with status (Sent, Delivered, Failed, Read).
  • Manual Override: Admin can manually trigger or postpone reminders.
  • Reminder History: Complete audit trail of all communications.
4.5 Service History & Reports
  • Customer History: Detailed chronological view of all services for each customer including date, technician, complaint, actions taken, parts used, cost and signatures.
  • Service Analysis: Frequency analysis, average service interval per customer, top recurring issues, and suggestions for preventive action.
  • Revenue Reports: Daily, weekly, monthly, quarterly and annual revenue tracking with filtering by service type or technician.
  • Technician Performance: Metric dashboards showing services completed, average job duration, customer feedback ratings, and punctuality.
  • Inventory Reports: Track stock levels for spare parts and consumables, get reorder alerts when items reach threshold, and view part-wise consumption by service type.
  • Export & Print: Export reports as PDF, Excel (CSV/XLSX) and print-friendly pages. Include options to generate consolidated monthly invoices and service summaries for customers or corporate clients.
  • Report Scheduling: Ability to schedule recurring reports (daily/weekly/monthly) to be emailed or downloaded automatically by admins.
  • Filtering & Drill-down: Interactive filters to drill down by date ranges, technician, service type, area, and filter model to analyze trends.
4.6 Print & Export Options
  • Printable Job Cards: Clean, branded templates for service orders that include signatures and photo thumbnails.
  • Invoice Generation: Create invoices from completed job cards with tax calculations and print/export options.
  • Bulk Export: Export multiple job cards or customer lists in a single operation.
  • Audit Trail: Include metadata (generated by, date/time) on all exported/printed documents for compliance and traceability.
4.7 User Management
  • Roles & Permissions: Admin and Technician roles with fine-grained permissions (create, read, update, delete).
  • User Lifecycle: Admins can add, suspend, or delete users; password reset flows; multi-factor authentication as an option.
  • Activity Logs: Record user actions (login, modifications to customer records, service order updates) for auditing.
  • Profile Settings: Users can update their profile, contact number, and working area preferences.

6 System Architecture Overview

The system follows a layered architecture to separate concerns and make the application maintainable and scalable.

Components
  • Frontend Layer: Web Admin Dashboard (React/Next.js) and Mobile interface for technicians (React Native or Flutter). Responsive and PWA-ready for quick installs.
  • Backend / API Layer: RESTful APIs (Node.js/Express or Django REST Framework), authentication & authorization, scheduled jobs for reminders, and integrations (WhatsApp API, SMS Gateway).
  • Database Layer: Relational DB for structured data (PostgreSQL/MySQL) and optional MongoDB for flexible records (e.g., audit logs, photos metadata).
  • Reporting & Printing Layer: PDF generation service using jsPDF/PDFKit and ExcelJS for exports; background workers to generate heavy reports.
  • Integration Components: WhatsApp Business API connector, SMS gateway, Email service (SES/SendGrid), and payment gateway integration for invoice payments.
  • Offline Sync (Optional): Local DB storage on mobile (SQLite/Realm) with sync engine to resolve conflicts when connection is restored.
Suggested Deployment Pattern

Use containerized deployments (Docker) with orchestration (Kubernetes / managed containers) or use managed app services on DigitalOcean / AWS ECS for smaller teams. CDN for static assets and object storage (S3/Spaces) for photos and backups.
